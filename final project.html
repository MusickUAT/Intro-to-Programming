War Lengths and Casualties

War Length of Time Casualties
American Civil War April 1861 to April 1865, around 4 years 618,222 deaths, 360,222 being Union and 258,000 being Confederate
Spanish-American War April 1898 to December 1898, around 8 months 2061 American Soldiers, unknown Spanish soldiers
World War I aka "The Great War" July 1914 to November 1918, around 4 years 4 months 19.7 million, 9.7 million being soldiers, 10 million being civilians
World War II September 1939 to September 1946, around 7 years 53 million casualties, 15 million soldiers, 38 million civilians
Vietnam War November 1955 to April 1975, almost 20 years 2,450,000 casualties, 1,062,000 deaths being US/South Vietnamese, 741,000 being North Vietnamese
